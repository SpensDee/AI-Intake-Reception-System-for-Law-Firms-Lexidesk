FAQ

Common Questions

What is AI intake software for law firms?

AI intake software automates answering inbound calls, qualifying legal leads, booking consultations, and following up with prospects — replacing or supplementing traditional receptionists and call centers.

How does Lexidesk handle after-hours calls?

Lexidesk's AI voice agent runs 24/7 including evenings, weekends, and holidays. It conducts structured intake interviews, qualifies leads, and books consultations automatically.

Which practice areas does Lexidesk support?

Family Law, Personal Injury, Immigration, Criminal Defense, Estate Planning, Probate, Employment Law, and Conveyancing / Real Estate Law.

Does Lexidesk integrate with legal CRMs?

Yes — Clio Grow, Clio Manage, Lawmatics, HubSpot, Neos, Zapier, Make, and n8n. Lead data, transcripts, and scores sync automatically.

How long does onboarding take?

Standard onboarding is 3–5 business days, including intake workflow setup, AI scripting, and CRM integration. Two weeks of unlimited tweaks are included post-launch.

What firm sizes does Lexidesk work with?

Solo attorneys through large firms with 200+ lawyers. The platform scales to call volume and can handle overflow for busy practices.
