Lexidesk is an AI-powered legal intake and reception platform built exclusively for consumer-facing law firms. The platform automates phone intake, website chat, lead qualification, consultation booking, follow-up sequences, and legal client routing using advanced conversational AI.

Lexidesk helps law firms answer calls 24/7, capture more qualified leads, reduce missed opportunities, and replace or supplement traditional legal receptionists and call centers.

# What Lexidesk Does

Lexidesk replaces or supplements traditional legal receptionists and call centers by using AI-powered voice and chat agents that handle inbound leads from first contact to consultation booking.

The platform can:

* Conduct full legal intake interviews over phone and website chat
* Qualify legal leads using firm-specific qualification criteria
* Score leads from 1–10 based on case quality and urgency
* Book free or paid consultations automatically
* Send SMS and email scheduling links
* Transfer qualified callers directly to attorneys during business hours
* Handle after-hours calls and overflow intake automatically
* Send personalized follow-up sequences to unconverted leads
* Route legal inquiries to the correct team or department
* Refer unqualified leads to partner law firms
